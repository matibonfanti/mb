interesting reads

papers, books, essays i find interesting, insightful and relevant

  • Leopold Aschenbrenner's Situational Awareness: the Decade Ahead - an ex-openai employee's perspective on the future outlook of ai. very much disagree with his risk assessment and his strategic geopolitical suggestions but it's a (somewhat technical) deep dive by a very smart insider.
  • Mandelbrot's "The (mis)Behaviour of Markets: a Fractal View of Risk, Ruin, and Reward" - chanllenges the assumptions of gaussian ditributions and efficient markets. applies fractal geometry to markets, shows that they exhibit self-similarity. for more technical stuff see "The Variation of Certain Speculative Prices" (1963) and "A Multifractal Model of Asset Returns" (1997)
